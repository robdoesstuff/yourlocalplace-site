Frequently Asked Questions

Why did I get an email from @yourlocal.place?
A local business you've interacted with used our platform to send you a personalized offer or update. The email came from businessname@yourlocal.place — that's how businesses on our platform reach their customers.
What is checkin.yourlocal.place?
checkin.yourlocal.place is a front-desk check-in page for local businesses. If you were asked to visit this URL, the business is using our platform to welcome and track visits so they can send you great offers later.
Is this spam?
No. Every message sent through our platform requires your prior opt-in consent. You agreed to hear from this business — likely when you checked in, made a purchase, or signed up at their location. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS or clicking unsubscribe in any email.
Who runs yourlocal.place?
yourlocal.place is owned and operated by horoco LLC, a software company that builds tools for local businesses. Our flagship product is jomo, the AI agent that helps businesses bring back their best customers.
I'm a business owner — how do I get started?
Head over to thejomoapp.com to learn about jomo and start creating instant offers for your customers. It's AI-powered, easy to set up, and built to fill seats during your quietest hours.
How do I stop getting messages?
For SMS, reply STOP to any message. For email, click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any message. You'll be removed immediately.
