Frequently Asked Questions

Why did I get a message from @yourlocal.place?
You opted in at a participating local location to receive instant offers, and yourlocal.place sent you a time-sensitive deal for that spot. Messages are sent from locationname@yourlocal.place — that's how yourlocal.place identifies which participating location an offer is for.
What is checkin.yourlocal.place?
checkin.yourlocal.place is a front-desk check-in page that participating local locations use to welcome customers. If you were asked to visit this URL, you're checking in at a spot supported by yourlocal.place so we can send you great offers for that location later.
Is this spam?
No. Every message from yourlocal.place requires your prior opt-in consent. You agreed to receive instant offers from yourlocal.place for a participating location — likely when you checked in, made a purchase, or signed up at that spot. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS or clicking unsubscribe in any email.
Who runs yourlocal.place?
yourlocal.place is owned and operated by horoco LLC, a software company that builds tools for local businesses. Our flagship product is jomo, the AI agent that powers the instant offers yourlocal.place delivers for participating locations.
I run a local spot — how do I get started?
Head over to thejomoapp.com to learn about jomo and enroll your location with yourlocal.place. We'll help you put time-sensitive offers in front of the customers who already love your spot — AI-powered, easy to set up, and built to fill seats during your quietest hours.
How do I stop getting messages?
For SMS, reply STOP to any message. For email, click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any message. You'll be removed immediately.
